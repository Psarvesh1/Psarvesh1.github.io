Chymey

It is an tea product selling e-commerce platform.

This project is complete modern e-commerce website, where customers can buy all variety of tea. It is purely made in React.js and used MongoDB (NoSql) for database. Hosted the database and website completely on aws. Added razor payment gateway to complete the purchase.
